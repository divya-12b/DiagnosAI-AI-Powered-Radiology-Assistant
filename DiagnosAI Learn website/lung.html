Lung Diseases

Learn about common lung infections and conditions.

Viral Pneumonia

About: Viral pneumonia can result from influenza, respiratory syncytial virus (RSV), or other viruses. Symptoms include cough, fever, fatigue, muscle aches, and sometimes difficulty breathing. Children, older adults, and immunocompromised individuals are more susceptible.

Prevention & Treatment:

  • Vaccinations (e.g., flu shots) help reduce risk.
  • Supportive care: rest, hydration, fever control, and antivirals if indicated.
  • Hospitalization may be needed for severe cases or high-risk patients.

Do's & Don'ts:

  • DO practice good hand hygiene and wear masks if recommended.
  • DO seek medical help if breathing difficulties worsen.
  • DON’T ignore persistent high fever or signs of dehydration.

Corona Virus (COVID-19)

About: Caused by SARS-CoV-2, COVID-19 symptoms can range from mild (fever, cough, loss of taste/smell) to severe (pneumonia, acute respiratory distress). Some individuals experience long-term effects (“long COVID”) even after initial recovery.

Prevention & Treatment:

  • Vaccination, masking, and social distancing can reduce spread.
  • Supportive care at home for mild cases; hospitalization for severe cases.
  • Antiviral medications (e.g., Paxlovid) or monoclonal antibodies for high-risk patients.

Do's & Don'ts:

  • DO follow local public health guidelines and get tested if symptomatic.
  • DO stay hydrated and rest; monitor oxygen levels if recommended.
  • DON’T dismiss persistent symptoms; seek medical attention if they worsen.

Normal Scan

About: A normal lung scan indicates no detectable infection, tumor, or structural issue. However, ongoing symptoms like shortness of breath or a chronic cough may require additional investigations (e.g., spirometry, chest X-ray, or referral to a pulmonologist).

Respiratory Health Tips:

  • Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke to reduce lung damage.
  • Engage in regular aerobic exercise (walking, jogging, swimming) to strengthen lung capacity.
  • Maintain good indoor air quality (use air purifiers, ensure proper ventilation).
